Privacy Policy for Flossi

Last updated: October 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Flossi, a personal finance and money management app designed to help you track expenses, split bills, and manage shared wallets easily. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the “Service”).

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, and phone number when you register for an account.
  • Financial Data: Information you input about your transactions, spending categories, and shared wallets.
  • Device Information: Includes device type, operating system, app version, and unique identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Data on how you use the app, such as features accessed and session duration.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Flossi app functionality.
  • To personalize your experience and improve our features.
  • To analyze usage trends and enhance performance.
  • To communicate with you about updates, offers, or security issues.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who help operate the app (e.g., analytics, hosting, or payment processors).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from loss, theft, and misuse through encryption, secure servers, and restricted access. However, no system can be 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can exercise these rights through the app settings or by contacting us directly.

7. Children’s Privacy

Flossi is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe your child has provided information, please contact us to remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app, and the updated date will be revised accordingly.
